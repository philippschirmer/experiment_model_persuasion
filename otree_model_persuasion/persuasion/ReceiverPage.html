You are the {{ player.role }}. In this role, you will be playing a financial client who can either buy or sell stocks. You will be presented with different financial charts that show the development of stock prices for a given time period.

You will have to make your decision by trying your best at predicting the stock price in the future. If you predict that the stock price is going to increase, then the best strategy is to buy the stock at the current price and then sell the stock in the future to gain profit. On the other hand, if you predict the stock price is going to fall in the future, then the best option is to sell the stock now and buy it later to gain aditional revenue.

In a later step of the game however, you will be presented with some models provided by financial advisers - this is, you won't have to predict the price by yourself but rather choose the model that you think fits the data better.

In the next step, you will be answering some general comprehension questions, to check whether you fully understand your role in the game.
