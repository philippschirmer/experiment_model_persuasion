Instructions

Welcome and thank you for participating in this experiment! In this experiment, you will encounter chart of randomly generated data that resembles stock prices. Throughout the game, you will make decisions for a series of such graphs, where the exact decision depend on the role you are assigned. In addition to a fixed show-up fee of {{C.SHOW_UP_FEE}}, you can earn additional rewards depending on your decisions throughout the game. You cannot lose any rewards. At the beginning of the game, you will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant, where one of you will take the role of a financial advisor and the other a financial customer. The financial customer is faced with the decision of buying or selling a stock after observing a stock. The financial advisor is tasked with providing advice to the financial customer. Seeing the same financial charts as the customer, the advisor can draw a message into the graph to argue for the future trend of the stock.

Prior to making your decision, you will learn your role, which will be randomly assigned.
